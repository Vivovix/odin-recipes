Agua di Valencia

Description

Valencian Water (Valencian, Aigua de València.) is a cocktail made from a base of cava or champagne, orange juice, vodka and gin. In general, it is served in pitchers of various sizes and is consumed from a broad cocktail glass. It was made for the first time in 1959 by Constante Gil in the bar Café Madrid de Valencia in the city of Valencia, Spain.

source: Wikipedia

Ingredients

  • 500 ml orange juice freshly squeezed
  • 300 ml cava semisweet sparkling wine- chilled
  • 100 ml vodka
  • 100 ml gin

Steps

  1. Mix together all ingredients in a 1 liter pitcher or carafe.
  2. Serve in champagne or martini cocktail glasses.
